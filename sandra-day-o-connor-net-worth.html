Sandra Day O'Connor Net Worth

Sandra Day O'Connor was a groundbreaking figure in American history, becoming the first woman to serve on the United States Supreme Court. Born on March 26, 1930 in El Paso, TX, she was appointed to the position by President Ronald Reagan in 1981 and served until 2006. Her appointment was a major milestone in the fight for gender equality in the United States.
