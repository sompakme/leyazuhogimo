Stings Final Match At AEW Revolution Seemingly Set During 1/10 Dynamite

Sting’s final match is going down at AEW Revolution on March 3rd. That match will be a historic event, but he needs an opponent. It appears that a tag team match has now been set.

The Young Bucks haven’t been seen for a long time, but that hiatus ended this week at Daily’s Place. After the main event match, The Young Bucks ended their hiatus.

Sting and Darby Allin won a tag team match against Konosuke Takeshita and Powerhouse Hobbs, and the Tony Schiavone asked Sting who he will face in his final match. That is when The Young Bucks upstaged The Icon.

The Young Bucks came out, and they had mustaches. We will have to see if reports come out that those mustaches were influenced by Vince McMahon’s new look. That was quite a change in their look, and they just stood there and looked menacing before leaving.

It was noted that The Young Bucks were calling out Sting and Darby Allin for Revolution. Although the match wasn’t booked, it was heavily implied. We will have more updates for you here at Ringside News.
