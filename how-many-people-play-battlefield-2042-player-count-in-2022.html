How many people play Battlefield 2042? Player count in 2022

Wondering if people still play Battlefield 2042? Let's go over the average Battlefield 2042 player count.

First Published November 23, 2022, 11:18

EA's Battlefield 2042 is a new first-person shooter with mixed reviews from its players. Some fans absolutely love the game, while others feel that it has not received enough updates since its release. 

With so many mixed feelings about Battlefield 2042 plastered all over social media, many players wonder if people still play the game, or if it is now dead. Let's go over the average BF 2042 player count in 2022.

Battlefield 2042 Player Count (2022)

The average Battlefield 2042 player count in the last 30 days is 3,708.4 players. The average each month in the past year ranged from about 2,000 to just under 8,000.

That said, the player count saw a huge spike on 22nd November 2022. As many as almost 14,000 players were online at one time today, with the player count reaching a peak of 13,788. Peak players had not been this high since August 2022, when the game saw a high of 16,856, its highest since release.

Is Battlefield 2042 Player Count Incr

The average Battlefield 2042 player count has gone up and down multiple times over the past year, with large fluctuations throughout the year, so it can be hard to say whether the player count will increase or decrease in the long run.

That said, the game has seen one of its highest recent peaks in players thanks to its 22nd November 2022 release on Xbox Game Pass and the start of a new season, Battlefield 2042 Season 3. As such, it's likely that players can expect at least a small influx of new players in the near future.

Yes, Battlefield 2042 had a pretty rocky release, with many players expressing their disappointment with some aspects of the game and the lack of updates. This first-person shooter is starting to find its footing, though, with a large influx of players coming in after its release on Xbox Game Pass and the start of Season 3.

That's all you need to know about Battlefield 2042's player count in 2022, including how many players participate in the game each month on average, and whether or not players should expect an influx of newbies.
