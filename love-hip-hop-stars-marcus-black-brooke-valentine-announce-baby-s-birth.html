&quot;Love &amp; Hip Hop&quot; Stars Marcus Black &amp; Brooke Valentine Announce Baby's Birth

Another Love & Hip Hop couple has quietly welcomed a baby girl into their lives. We can always count on the VH1 show to amp up the drama but there are also plenty of wholesome moments that take place behind-the-scenes and on screen. Marcus Black and Brooke Valentine told the world that they were engaged to be married a while ago and now, it's become apparent that they've taken a huge jump in their relationship. Marcus wrote a thank-you note to his woman on social media yesterday to celebrate Mother's Day and in doing so, he announced the birth of their baby girl.

Marcus Black and Brooke Valentine are welcoming their daughter into the world and the news was shared with us all via Instagram. Black wrote, "I went back and forth all day on whether or not I was gon post this but I mean... it’s Mother’s Day babe." The decision to reveal their daughter's arrival was clearly eating away at Black. "I just can’t let the day end without publicly thanking you for our princess," he revealed. "I thank God for you and I’m excited about this new journey. Through all the ups and downs, high risk appointments, hospital stays and life’s uncertainties you stayed solid. You’ve always been ten toes down. I never had to question where you stood in my life. My plan is to multiply your investment in my purpose. I love you without conditions my n***a... Happy Mother’s Day."
