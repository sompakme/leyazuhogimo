Marie Lodi - InStyle

Title: Freelance Writer

Marie Lodi is an LA-based writer and editor covering beauty, fashion, and costume design for 15 years. She co-hosts Fishnet Flix, a podcast about fashion in film, and is the Looks Editor for Bust Magazine. Previously, she was the Senior Beauty Editor at HelloGiggles and the Style Advice Columnist at Rookie. Her bylines have appeared in The Cut, PAPER, Bustle, Refinery29, Fashionista, Allure, Buzzfeed News, and more. You can find Marie Lodi on Instagram: @marielodiandreakos.

Experience

Marie has written about everything from Botox to witchcraft, and interviewed a wide range of celebs and famous figures from Christina Aguilera and Katy Perry to John Waters and Elvira.
