Amanda Seyfried Is Striking in a Lime Green Minidress at the SAG Awards

Amanda Seyfried is a vision in lime green at tonight's Screen Actors Guild Awards.

The actress walked the red carpet this Sunday in a silky minidress featuring a long train and spaghetti straps accented with tiny bows. She paired the piece, custom-made by Prada, with strappy black sandals. She further accessorized with dazzling Cartier jewelry, including diamond and emerald drop earrings, which color coordinated with the dress, and a diamond bracelet.

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

Seyfried finished the look—styled by Elizabeth Stewart—with some dreamy glam: a wavy half-up, half-down hairdo, rosy eyeshadow and blush, a pinkish nude lip, and inky black eyeliner.

The 29th annual SAG Awards took place this Sunday, February 26, at the Fairmont Century Plaza in Los Angeles.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

Seyfried was nominated in the category of Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Television Movie or Limited Series for her lead role in the Hulu series The Dropout. In the acclaimed show, she plays Elizabeth Holmes, the woman who founded Theranos, a now-defunct health technology company, and was convicted for fraud. The miniseries premiered in March 2022.

The Dropout received another nod on Saturday, when it was given the David L. Wolper Award for Outstanding Producer of Limited or Anthology Series Television at the 34th Annual Producers Guild Awards.

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

Seyfried ended up losing the award tonight to Jessica Chastain, who was nominated for her role as Tammy Wynette in George and Tammy. Chastain made sure to praise fellow nominee Seyfried in her acceptance speech.
