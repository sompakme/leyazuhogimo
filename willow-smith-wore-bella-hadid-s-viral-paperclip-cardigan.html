Willow Smith Wore Bella Hadid's Viral Paperclip Cardigan

Willow Smith is taking a page out of Bella Hadid's chaotic fashion handbook. On Sunday, the singer took to Instagram to show off her latest outfit, which included the viral paperclip top originally worn by the model.

Willow posed in a tiny, neon green cardigan fastened together by a piece of gold hardware inscribed with the name of the designer, Jacquemus, which she matched to her lace thong that peeked out of her black miniskirt. She paired the underboob- and ab-baring ensemble with a matching leather trench coat and knee-high boots while accessorizing with a gray beanie, massive square-framed shades, gold hoop earrings, and a matching chain necklace.

"It's a <vibe.com>," Willow wrote alongside the two selfies that displayed her ensemble. She also tagged her stylists, Chloe and Chenelle Delgadillo, who presumably crafted the early aughts-inspired outfit.

Bella initially wore the sexy shawl last year in an Instagram carousel tagging her friend and designer, Simon Porte Jacquemus, who designed the piece that would soon be plastered all over the internet, launching a full-blown obsession with the paperclip top.

"@jacquemus the male version of me 😂🫀🥐🌺🌻🌼☘️💐🌼🕊🐝🐣👒😈," she captioned the gallery that featured mirror snaps, artsy videos, and a photo of Jacquemus lounging on a long suede couch. Bella's version of the top came in a hot pink hue that she offset with beige, oversized cargo pants.
