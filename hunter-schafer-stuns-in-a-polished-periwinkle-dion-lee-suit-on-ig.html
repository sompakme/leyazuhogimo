Hunter Schafer Stuns in a Polished Periwinkle Dion Lee Suit on IG

Hunter Schafer is making a name for herself in the fashion industry.

Yesterday, the Euphoria actress posted a series of photos to Instagram, featuring a periwinkle ensemble from Australian womenswear brand Dion Lee. The three-piece outfit included a well-tailored suit with a lightweight knit of the same color underneath. Showing off the look in a compilation of three shots, she stayed true to herself by adding a pair of thigh-high boots with shearling lining.

Also in true Schafer style, she matched her fashion look to her glam, lining her lower lids with a light purple eye shadow by Japanese beauty brand Shiseido. The model sported a peachy-pink color on the lip to complement the soft hues. Bringing the full idea together, she wore her hair slicked down the middle with the ends flipped up.

The young star has become somewhat of a fashion savant since landing on the scene. Between wearing colorful cutout looks by LVMH Prize finalist Rui Zhou and iconic pieces from French house Schiaparelli, Schafer has proved her range over the years. The 22 year-old has also had her fair share of appearances on the runways, including Mugler's spring/summer 2021 virtual runway alongside Bella Hadid and Pose star Dominique Jackson.

The star-studded fashion event, quite literally, made a splash on social media with a super-sexy lineup of celebrities and models sporting the latest collection from Casey Cadwallader. Schafer sported the "wet" look in an off-the-shoulder, sheer midi dress with an iridescent finish, which clung to the body for a second-skin fit on the catwalk.
