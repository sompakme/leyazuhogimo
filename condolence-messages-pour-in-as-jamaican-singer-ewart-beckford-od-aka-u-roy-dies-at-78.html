Condolence messages pour in as Jamaican singer Ewart Beckford OD aka U-Roy dies at 78

- Veteran Jamaican singer and vocalist, Ewart Beckford OD, has been reported dead at the age of 78

- Fans of the late musician also known as U-Roy have taken to social media to mourn his passing

- Pictures and videos of the deceased musician were also posted on social media

It is indeed a difficult time for the family members of veteran Jamaican singer and vocalist, Ewart Beckford OD, who has just left the world.

The musician who hails from the Kingston area of Jamaica died at the age of 78 on Wednesday, February 17, Dead Death reports.

Details about the cause of death remain unknown but it has not stopped fans and lovers of the late musician also known as U-Roy from mourning him on social media.
