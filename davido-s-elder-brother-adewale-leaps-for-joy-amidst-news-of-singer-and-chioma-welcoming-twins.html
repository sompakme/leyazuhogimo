Davidos Elder Brother Adewale Leaps for Joy Amidst News of Singer and Chioma Welcoming Twins

  • Adewale Adeleke, the brother of Nigerian singer Davido, gave fans a clue about the celebration going down in their family
  • Recall that there have been speculations about the DMW boss and his wife, Chioma Rowland, recently welcoming twins
  • While neither Davido nor his wife have officially confirmed the news, the singer's elder brother gave thanks to God

Nigerian singer Davido's brother, Adewale Adeleke, may have confirmed reports that the singer recently welcomed a set of twins.

Legit.ng reported that the DMW boss and his wife, Chioma Rowland, were alleged to have recently given birth to twins.

Neither Davido nor his wife have yet to directly confirm the rumours.

However, the musician's elder brother Adewale gave fans and netizens another clue on the great news as he took to social media to express gratitude to God while the rumour was circulating.
