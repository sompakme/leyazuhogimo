Who is Rhea Seehorn? age

Rhea Seehorn is an American actress popularly known for her role as Kim Wexler in the television crime series. Better call Saul . She previously played roles on NBC Whitney ABC I’m With Her and TNT Franklin & Bash . She was born on May 12, 1972 in Norfolk, Virginia and has lived in different cities while growing up, but currently resides in New York.

Her interest in art was all inspired by her father and grandma, who were art lovers and lived not far away, decided to paint, draw and do architectural designs by them. During her studies she became interested in acting and became acquainted with contemporary theatre. She later earned a bachelor’s degree in art from George Mason University in 1994.

Meanwhile, determining a career choice for the actress was not an easy task as she was considering whether to become an artist or an actress. She later made up her mind after her father died and left her a note insisting that she pursue any dream she wanted, prompting Rhea to take her first bold steps as an actress starring in the film occurred. A Case Against Karen (1998), where she starred as Shari. The following year she made yet another outstanding performance in the film, Why Spain? and subsequently acted as a pitcher in the short film, The Pitch and also in The Gentleman (2000) in the same year .Other films she has acted in include I’ll Be With Her (2003), Shaggy Dog (2006), Whitney (2011), Inside Man2 (2018) and a few others.

In addition to her roles in feature films, Rhea Seehorn has appeared in a number of other television series and shows. She currently stars as Kim Wexler in the crime series, Better Call Saul . Her other television roles include: I’ll Be With Her (aired 2003-2004), Main Cases (2005), Romy and Michele: In the Beginning (2005), The Singles Table (2007) and so many others.
