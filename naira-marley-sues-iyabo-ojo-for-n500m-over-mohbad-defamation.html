Naira Marley Sues Iyabo Ojo for N500M Over Mohbad Defamation

In a surprising turn of events, Nigerian singer Naira Marley has demanded a public apology from popular actress Iyabo Ojo, accusing her of defamation and threatening to sue for damages amounting to N500 million.

The controversy stems from the unfortunate demise of Mohbad, a rising star in the music industry. Naira Marley, who had been vocal about seeking justice for Mohbad, expressed his disappointment in Iyabo Ojo‘s alleged defamatory statements related to the case.

Despite widespread public outcry for justice, Naira Marley claims that Iyabo Ojo‘s remarks have tarnished his reputation and further complicated the situation surrounding Mohbad‘s death. In a social media post, the singer insisted that Iyabo Ojo and the late artist owe him a public apology for her statements.

The singer’s legal team has reportedly issued a notice of intention to sue, with the lawsuit seeking damages totaling N500 million. The lawsuit alleges that Iyabo Ojo‘s comments have caused significant harm to Naira Marley‘s image and career.

The controversy surrounding Mohbad‘s death initially garnered attention on social media platforms, with claims of the deceased haunting individuals in their dreams. However, as time passed, the case faced setbacks, including a lack of autopsy results, arrests, or updates from the authorities.

Naira Marley, known for his outspoken nature, has called out both the Nigerian Police Force and officer Benjamin Hundeyin, accusing them of not keeping their word and failing to provide justice in Mohbad‘s case.

The hashtag #JusticeForMohbad gained momentum on social media, reflecting the public’s frustration with the lack of progress in the investigation. Now, Naira Marley’s legal actions against Iyabo Ojo add a new layer to the ongoing saga surrounding Mohbad‘s untimely death.

As the legal proceedings unfold, the Nigerian entertainment industry and the public await resolving this unexpected and dramatic turn of events.
