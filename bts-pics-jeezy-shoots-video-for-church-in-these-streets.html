BTS Pics: Jeezy Shoots Video For &quot;Church In These Streets&quot;

To promote his upcoming 8th studio album Church In These Streets, which drops November 13, Young Jeezy decided to shoot a new music video earlier this week in its support for the previously heard title track.

From the few pics that surfaced online (see gallery above), the video was shot in Atlanta on Thursday and looks to have Jeezy dressed in Sunday’s best as he visits mass & preaches to the people. No word yet as for when the official video will arrive, but it should be dropping sooner than later. Look for a cameo from Boosie Badazz too.

In other news, look for Jeezy to continue his weekly #SundayService series this weekend when he releases latest sermon in two days. Hell, maybe it’s this video?

Check out the BTS pics in the gallery above, and keep your eyes peeled for Sunday’s new release.
