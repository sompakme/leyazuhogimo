Giusy Meloni, the beauty that has caused an earthquake in the Italian League

Giusy Meloni, born in 1999, started her career as an actress and then became a successful TV presenter. But it was earlier this year when A.C. Milan of the Italian League signed the hostess for its TV channel, leaving the club's fans amazed by her beauty. She is a lover of good food, of exploring new destinations through unimaginable trips and of her passion for soccer, although she has never made it clear which team she is a supporter of. She is, however, a big fan of the Italian national team, which she hopes to see at the Euro 2024 in Germany, defending the title.

Giusy Meloni has been an integral part of the Italian TV sports program, La Domenica Sportiva, where she has skillfully navigated the complex world of sports reporting. Her prowess as a sports presenter goes further, as she also took on the role of host of Milan Talk, a dedicated program covering the ups and downs of AC Milan's men's and women's teams.

Giusy has an important social profile with almost 300,000 followers. Since this summer, her publications have had an increase in likes and interactions, which has led her to be defined as a social icon. Among her most popular snapshots are selfies and costume photos. But her popularity goes beyond club boundaries, as fans of rival clubs share their affection for Giusy. One said, "If you supported Juve you would be perfect." Another wrote: "You make me doubt whether I'm an Inter fan."
