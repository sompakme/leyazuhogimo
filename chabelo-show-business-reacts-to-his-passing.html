Chabelo: Show business reacts to his passing

The world of comedy is in mourning after it was announced that Xavier Chabelo Lopez died at the age of 88 due to health complications on Saturday.

The family of the actor, host and comedian announced the news of his passing on Twitter.

"This is a very sad morning, Chabelo, father, brother and husband has left us suddenly due to abdominal complications.

"With our souls torn apart and knowing that many people have loved him for many years and that they will feel his departure, we ask you to pray for his peaceful rest and to give us the opportunity to mourn. Lopez Miranda family," the Twitter post read.

The world of showbusiness has reacted to Chabelo's death

After the news of the comedian's death, several personalities from celebrities to politicians have left messages for "El amigo de todos los ninos" (The friend of all children).

"I would like to extend a big hug to the family and friends of Chabelo after his passing," Mexican President Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador declared.

"How could I forget that my eldest son used to wake up early to see him more than 40 years ago."

"The man who filled us with laughter, the one who brought families together in front of the television to share joy. My dear Chabelo, thank you for making so many generations remember the child inside us," wrote journalist Adela Micha.

"Sadly, it is true now. The friend of all children is gone. Thanks for everything dear Chabelo RIP", shared businessman Arturo Elias Ayub.

"He was a reference in Mexican television. He marked an era and his story is in the hearts of many generations who enjoyed him every Sunday morning in their homes with his quiz show. May the great actor and comedian, EL ETERNO NINO, Xavier Lopez Chabelo, rest in peace," said journalist Jose Ramon Fernandez.

"Sad news of the passing of Xavier Lopez "Chabelo", condolences to family, friends and fans. Rest in peace," wrote Foreign Minister Marcelo Ebrard.
