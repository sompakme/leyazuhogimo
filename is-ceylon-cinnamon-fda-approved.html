Is it safe to take Ceylon cinnamon?

When taken by mouth: Ceylon cinnamon is commonly consumed in foods. It is possibly safe when used as a medicine. It has been safely used in doses of 0.5-3 grams daily for up to 6 months. But Ceylon cinnamon is possibly unsafe when taken in larger amounts or when used long-term.

What is the safest cinnamon to take?

Both Ceylon and cassia are healthy and delicious. However, if you intend to consume large amounts of this spice or take a supplement, cassia can be harmful because of the coumarin content. At the end of the day, Ceylon cinnamon is better quality and much safer.

How can you tell if Ceylon cinnamon is real?

As far as the texture or the feel is concerned, Ceylon Cinnamon is thin and papery and forms multiple layers when rolled up. In contrast, Cassia Cinnamon has a rougher thick bark which forms just a few layers when rolled up.
