Taylor Swift shares photos for her holiday in Hawaii

Taylor Swift has been enjoying a well-earned holiday in Hawaii. The singer, who is due to perform at the Grammys and the Brit Awards before heading off on her 1989 world tour, has been soaking up some sun with American rock trio Haim."Greetings from Maui! @haimtheband," Taylor captioned a photo of herself with singing sisters Este, Danielle and Alana Haim, posing in bikinis.

Taylor is holidaying with musical sisters Haim

"Este-back ride," she wrote alongside another swimsuit shot of herself in the sea with Este.The holiday hasn't just been fun and frolics on the beach, however, the quartet ventured out on a whale watching trip as well as taking a hike to enjoy Hawaii's natural beauty. "When we were whale watching and didn't see whales for 5 minutes and got all moody. @haimtheband," Taylor captioned an arty black-and-white shot of the group during their excursion.

On Saturday Taylor, Este, Danielle and Alana showed their appreciation for Bill Murray by uploading a snapshot of themselves wearing matching jumpers bearing the actor's name.Taylor added a quote by the Ghostbusters star as the caption, writing: ""I try to be available for life to happen to me." -Bill Murray."
