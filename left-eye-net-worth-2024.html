Left Eye’s Early Life and Career

Left Eye was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and began her music career in the late 1980s. She moved to Atlanta, Georgia, where she joined TLC, which would go on to become one of the best-selling girl groups of all time.

Success with TLC

With TLC, Left Eye achieved significant commercial success. The group’s second album, “CrazySexyCool,” sold over 14 million copies worldwide and earned them two Grammy Awards. Their third album, “FanMail,” also sold millions of copies and won them another Grammy.

Left Eye’s Solo Career

While still a member of TLC, Left Eye embarked on a solo career. Her solo album, “Supernova,” was released internationally but did not see a U.S. release. Despite this, the album was certified Gold by the British Phonographic Industry.

Posthumous Earnings
