Stonebwoy Joins Davido At His Sold Out Timeless Concert At The Madison Square Garden, Video

  • Nigerian musician Stonewboy brought Ghanaian musician Stonebwoy on stage at his sold-out concert at the Madison Square Garden in the U.S
  • A video of them performing their 2023 hit song Activate has gone viral on social media
  • Many people expressed their joy after the two musicians shared the stage

Grammy-nominated Nigerian musician Davido surprised his fans with a special performance with Ghanaian dancehall musician Stonebwoy.

Davido performs with Stonebwoy at the Madison Square Garden

Davido brought out Stonebwoy at his sold-out concert at the Madison Square Garden in New York City, United States of America.

The 19,500-capacity arena was filled with cheers and screams the moment Stonebwoy appeared on stage to perform their 2023 hit song Activate.
