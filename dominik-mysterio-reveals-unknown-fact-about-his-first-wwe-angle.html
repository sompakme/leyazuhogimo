Dominik Mysterio Reveals Unknown Fact About His First WWE Angle

Dominik Mysterio is one of the most hated heels in WWE today, but there was a time when Rey Mysterio’s son was just getting started, even before he was able to drive. Dominik was the centerpiece in a storyline with Rey Mysterio and Eddie Guerrero.

In one of those classic segments, a young Dominik Mysterio was seen playing a PSP backstage. That image became meme worthy, and now we know what game he was playing.

In a recent episode of Up Up Down Down, Dominik Mysterio was seem playing along with other Superstars. That is when Mansoor told Dom Dom that the game he was playing had controls like FIFA.

When Dominik Mysterio asked Mansoor why he made a FIFA reference, Mansoor said that was what Dom was playing in that classic WWE segment. Then Mysterio revealed that he was actually playing GTA.

Mansoor was a bit blown away that Dominik Mysterio was playing GTA: Liberty City Stories. That being said, he was glad that Dom Dom wasn’t playing Final Fantasy.

We will have to see what kind of memories Dominik Mysterio rakes up in the years to come. If anything, he’s certain to maintain a lot of attention in his current heel status. For more on this story, and many more, keep checking back with Ringside News.
