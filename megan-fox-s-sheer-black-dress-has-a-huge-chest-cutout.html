Megan Foxs Sheer Black Dress Has a Huge Chest Cutout

Megan Fox’s latest look puts a sultry spin on beachwear.

The Jennifer’s Body star shared photos from her latest photo shoot on Instagram today, debuting an unexpected beach outfit composed of a sheer black dress and an electric silver bikini. The long-sleeved bodycon mesh dress features a huge cutout on the chest and torso, revealing Fox’s abs and triangle bikini.

In the images, the actor poses on a beach, at times lounging halfway inside the ocean or crawling on the sand, like a sexy mermaid. For glam, she embraces the wet hair look, with her straight red hair entirely soaked. She wears bronzy, glowy makeup and a sleek black cat eye.

“Cliodhna, queen of the banshees,” she writes in the post caption.

The photos were taken by Cibelle Levi, the same photographer who shot Fox’s latest forest-fairy looks, which the actor also posted on Instagram. In some shots, Fox poses in a see-through asymmetric netted black dress adorned with purple butterfly appliqués—and no underwear.

In another set of images, she is climbing up a tree branch while wearing a barely-there lime-green string bikini. She wore the same purple butterflies and white hair extensions. “The forest is my oldest friend,” she captioned the pictures.

And in other photos, Fox is pictured posing almost completely nude while standing alongside a creek. She wears a white corset maxi dress with frilly lace trim and lace-up details on the bodice. The cottagecore mini is entirely soaked in water and unzipped in the back, revealing the actor’s naked body underneath.
