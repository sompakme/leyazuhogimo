James Lacey Dent (born May 9, 1939) is a professional golfer from the United States. Dent was born in Augusta, Georgia, the location of the Masters Tournament, despite the fact that as an African American, he would not have been permitted to play on the Augusta National course at the time, save as a caddy. As a kid, he caddied at Augusta National and Augusta Country Club. Dent went at Augusta’s Paine College, which was historically black. Dent began his professional career in 196He won the Florida PGA Championship three times throughout his normal (under 50) career. He is most known for his accomplishments on the Senior PGA Tour (now known as the Champions Tour), where he won 12 events between 1989 and 1998.
