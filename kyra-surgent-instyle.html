Kyra Surgent - InStyle

Location: New York

Kyra Surgent is a commerce writer at InStyle, where she covers fashion and beauty content and specializes in all things Amazon. Her work unearths the latest designer outlet deals and sales, reports on the hottest trends like breezy dresses and chunky “dad” sandals, reviews viral makeup, skincare, and hair care products, and covers celebrity style, including Meghan Markle, Jennifer Lopez, Jennifer Garner, and Kylie Jenner. She employs her personal passion for shopping and strong editorial background to identify the best buys for readers. 

She joined Dotdash Meredith in 2023. Prior to her current role, she contributed to Popsugar and served as an editor at Her Campus Media. When she's not searching (and shopping) the latest deals, you can find her practicing yoga, tracking down the best brunch in New York City, or partaking in the newest TikTok trend. Learn more about Kyra at her site.

Education

Kyra received a Bachelor of Science in Magazine, News and Digital Journalism with a concentration in fashion and beauty communications from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
