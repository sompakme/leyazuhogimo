What Happened To Johnny Manziel?

Johnny Manziel, once a celebrated college football star and Heisman Trophy winner, has had a tumultuous journey both on and off the field. His story is one of immense talent, high expectations, and a series of personal and professional setbacks that have left many wondering, “What happened to Johnny Manziel?” This article delves into the rise, fall, and attempts at redemption of a player who captured the nation’s attention and sparked debates about the pressures and pitfalls of early sports stardom.

Early Promise and College Stardom

Johnny Manziel’s football career began with a bang at Texas A&M University, where he earned the nickname “Johnny Football.” His dynamic play style and ability to make big plays in crucial moments quickly made him a fan favorite and a force to be reckoned with in college football.

  • First freshman to win the Heisman Trophy in 2012
  • Set numerous NCAA records, including becoming the first freshman to pass for 3,000 yards and rush for 1,000 yards in a season
  • Generated a massive following, with his games becoming must-watch TV for college football fans

The Transition to the NFL

Manziel’s success in college led to high expectations as he entered the NFL. Drafted by the Cleveland Browns in the first round of the 2014 NFL Draft, hopes were high that he would be the franchise quarterback the team had long been searching for.

  • 22nd overall pick in the 2014 NFL Draft
  • Generated buzz and excitement among Browns fans and the media
  • Faced immediate pressure to perform at a high level in the professional arena

Struggles in the NFL

However, Manziel’s transition to the NFL was anything but smooth. His performance on the field was inconsistent, and off-field issues began to overshadow his talent.

  • Struggled with the playbook and adapting to the NFL’s speed and complexity
  • Off-field antics and partying raised concerns about his commitment and focus
  • Started only eight games over two seasons with the Browns

Off-Field Controversies

Manziel’s off-field behavior became a significant distraction and a source of controversy. His partying lifestyle was well-documented, and he had several run-ins with the law.

  • Publicized incidents of partying during the season and in the offseason
  • Legal issues, including a domestic violence charge in 2016 (which was later dismissed)
  • Entered a rehabilitation treatment center in 2015 for undisclosed reasons

Release from the Cleveland Browns

The combination of poor performance and off-field issues led to Manziel’s release from the Browns in March 2016, just two years after being drafted.

  • Released after violating the team’s trust and failing to be the leader they needed
  • Left the NFL with a tarnished reputation and uncertain future

Attempts at a Comeback

Following his release from the Browns, Manziel attempted to revive his football career through various avenues.

  • Expressed a desire to return to the NFL and claimed to be sober and focused on football
  • Played in the Canadian Football League (CFL) for the Hamilton Tiger-Cats and Montreal Alouettes
  • Participated in the Alliance of American Football (AAF) for the Memphis Express before the league folded

Life After Football

As his football opportunities dwindled, Manziel began to focus on life beyond the gridiron.

  • Started to pursue educational opportunities and personal growth
  • Became more open about his struggles with mental health and substance abuse
  • Engaged in various business ventures and personal projects

Reflections on Fame and Personal Growth

Manziel has reflected publicly on his journey, acknowledging his mistakes and the pressures of fame at a young age.

  • Admitted to being unprepared for the spotlight and the responsibilities that came with it
  • Discussed the impact of his actions on his career and personal life
  • Expressed a desire to help others avoid similar pitfalls

Public Perception and Media Coverage

The media has played a significant role in shaping the public’s perception of Johnny Manziel.

  • Extensive coverage of his successes, failures, and personal life
  • Debates about the role of media scrutiny in his career trajectory
  • Continued interest in his story as a cautionary tale for young athletes

Johnny Manziel’s Legacy

Despite the ups and downs, Manziel’s legacy in football remains complex.

  • Remembered as one of the most electrifying college players of his time
  • His NFL career serves as a reminder of the challenges that professional athletes can face
  • Continues to be a topic of discussion when it comes to talent, potential, and the importance of personal development

Current Endeavors and Future Plans

Manziel has not completely closed the door on football but seems to be focusing on other aspects of his life.

  • Has hinted at potential involvement in coaching or mentoring young athletes
  • Exploring opportunities in sports media and commentary
  • Continues to work on personal development and maintaining a healthier lifestyle

FAQ Section

What is Johnny Manziel doing now?

Johnny Manziel is currently focusing on personal growth, education, and exploring business opportunities. He has also expressed interest in sports media and coaching roles.

Did Johnny Manziel ever play in the NFL again after being released by the Browns?

No, after being released by the Cleveland Browns, Johnny Manziel did not return to play in the NFL. He did, however, play in the Canadian Football League and the Alliance of American Football.

How does Johnny Manziel view his football career in hindsight?

Johnny Manziel has expressed regret over how he handled his early success and the opportunities he had. He acknowledges his lack of preparation for the pressures of fame and has spoken about learning from his experiences.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Johnny Manziel’s story is one of a meteoric rise to fame, followed by a rapid descent fueled by personal struggles and professional challenges. His journey serves as a powerful reminder of the pressures young athletes face and the importance of personal development alongside professional success. While his football career did not pan out as many had hoped, Manziel’s ongoing efforts to rebuild his life and find new paths forward offer a sense of hope and redemption. His legacy, though marred by controversy, also includes moments of brilliance that continue to resonate with fans of the sport.
