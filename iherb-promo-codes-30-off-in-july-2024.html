FAQs

Can I get free shipping from iHerb?

iHerb offers free standard shipping on all orders over $25. Some restrictions apply, so we recommend heading to the ‘Shipping’ page on the iHerb website where you can find a full list of restrictions, as well as alternative shipping methods.

Is there an iHerb welcome offer?

Yes, new iHerb customers will receive a 20% promo code to use on their first order. Simply enter your email address into the email address pop-up box to receive your exclusive welcome offer code. 

Can I sign up for the iHerb email newsletter?

Yes, as well as receiving a 20% discount on your first order, entering your email address is a great way to stay up to date with the latest news from iHerb. Be the first to know about the latest iHerb coupon codes, exclusive savings events, and other ways to save.

Can I shop the iHerb sale?

Yes, you can shop the iHerb sale all year round by heading to the ‘Sales and Offers’ page on the iHerb website. iHerb also runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and Christmas. In the past, customers have made seasonal savings of up to 40%. 

Does iHerb accept returns?

Yes of course. If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase then iHerb accepts returns made within 30 days of receipt for most name-brand products, and they accept returns made within 90 days for iHerb brand products. All items must be returned in their original packaging and in good condition, for you to be eligible for a full refund.
