Hardware

Here are the noteworthy Huawei Nova 6 details of the device's hardware.

Design

On the backside of the phone, at the top-left corner, there are three vertically aligned cameras. The camera setup also houses an LED flash for extra light when necessary. The power button and the volume rocker are positioned at the right side. The fingerprint sensor is also on the right side where you can easily find it with your fingers.

On the front side, the display panel is wide (approximately 6.57 inches); thanks to the thin bezels and a narrow chin. On the top left corner, there is an oval cutout which houses the dual system selfie camera. An earpiece which doubles as a speaker is at the top bezel. The phone weighs about 197g and thus, it is moderately heavy but easy to handle. Huawei Nova 6 colors are Black, Blue, Red and Provence. The design is sleek and eye-catching.

Display

The display technology that the phone uses is the LTPS IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen. The display panel is approximately 6.57 inches, which translate to about 104.2 cm2 with a screen resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels and a 20:9 aspect ratio.

The screen is wider compared to its predecessor, Huawei Nova 5, and it offers a better display unit perfect for gaming and watching videos. Additionally, you will not have problems viewing the contents of the screen in daylight because its sunlight legibility is good enough for clear viewing.

Processor

It comes with a HiSilicon Kirin 990 (7 nm) chipset, which is one of the best performing chipsets for Huawei phones. The chipset boasts of an octa-core (2x2.86 GHz Cortex-A76 & 2x2.09 GHz Cortex-A76 & 4x1.86 GHz Cortex-A55) CPU. The phone's graphics are supported by a Mali-G76 MP16 GPU.

With a RAM of 8GB, the processor can work optimally and facilitate the execution of various complex tasks such as gaming and multitasking. The specifications of the processor make it an ideal device for a user who intends to make intensive use of the phone.

Storage

The phone has an internal memory of 128 GB ROM and 8 GB RAM. It does not have an external memory card slot, and that means that you will have to be contented with the internal memory space only.

Battery

The device is packed with a non-removable Li-Po 4100 mAh battery, and that is one of the strengths of the phone. The high battery capacity is enough to power the operations of the phone for a considerable duration before recharging.

It also comes with 40W fast battery charging, which can charge the battery from 0% to 70% within 30 minutes. With the high battery capacity and fast charging, you can confidently use the device throughout the day.

Speaker and call quality

It has a main speaker that produces high-quality audio even at a high volume, which makes it suitable for listening to audio files. You can also opt to plug in the headphones in the 3.5mm jack and listen to audio files.

OS and features

The Huawei Nova 6 Android version 10.0, EMUI 10 is the most recent one. It offers an appealing user interface, and it supports various applications which improve the usability of the phone.

Huawei Nova 6 camera, video and image quality

The device has a tri-system rear camera with 40 MP (wide) + 8 MP (telephoto) + 8 MP (ultrawide) cameras. The front or selfie camera is a dual system with 32 MP (wide) + 8 MP (ultrawide) cameras. It is an ideal phone for photography and video recording, and it qualifies to be among the best camera phones today.
