Billy Gunn Returns During AEW Dynamite For All In Title Match

Billy Gunn left his boots in the ring during AEW Collision several weeks ago. That was a big surprise after a Trios Title match. That wasn’t the end of Billy Gunn’s career at all, and he made that very clear this week.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite this week.

The House of Black came out this week to jump The Acclaimed. Then when things looked bad, Billy Gunn made his way to the ring to declare his return.

After the WWE Hall of Famer cut a promo on the House of Black, they made it very clear that All In will be “all or nothing.” The Acclaimed and Daddy Ass want those Trios Titles from the House of Black, because there’s a Badass coming to London.
