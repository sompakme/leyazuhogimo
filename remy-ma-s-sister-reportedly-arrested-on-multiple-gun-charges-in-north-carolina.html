Remy Mas Sister Reportedly Arrested On Multiple Gun Charges In North Carolina

It appears Remy Ma’s younger sister is facing some serious legal trouble soon. According to a report by Bossip, Remeesha Blount, Remy Ma’s sister, was arrested at an apartment complex in North Carolina Monday (Feb. 19) after allegedly shooting a woman and firing into a vehicle full of three more women.

The shootout reportedly occurred last Friday in the parking lot of the End Zone sports bar in Raleigh, North Carolina. According to the News & Observer, the shooting victim was hit by a car as the three other women fled the scene in chaos. The unnamed victim is receiving treatment at Raleigh’s WakeMed Hospital where she is reportedly listed in fair condition.

A magistrate reportedly ordered Blount be held without bail based on a decision that she either would be unlikely to keep a court date or would be a danger to the community if she was freed. In addition, she also has pending charges from earlier arrests that include assault with a deadly weapon, shooting a gun inside city limits and assault by pointing a gun. So it’s clear she has a previous history with gun issues.

Blount currently faces multiple counts of assault with a deadly weapon with the intent to kill, as well as shooting at an occupied vehicle, and discharging a firearm in the city.

Her next scheduled court date is March 3rd. We’ll keep you posted on this developing story moving forward.
