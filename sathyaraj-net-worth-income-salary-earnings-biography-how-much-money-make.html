Sathyaraj (born Rangaraj Subbaiah on October 3, 1954) is an Indian film actor and media personality who has mostly featured in Tamil films. Sathyaraj was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He began his acting career as a villain and progressed to major parts later in his career. During the later half of his career, he mostly appeared in supporting and character parts, one of which was his portrayal of Periyar E. V. Ramasamy in the Tamil Nadu government-sponsored film Periyar. In addition, he was the director of the 1995 film Villadhi Villain, in which he played three distinct parts, including the title role. Apart from his cinema career, Sathyaraj has been outspoken in the media on a variety of topics, including Sri Lankan Tamil nationalism and water rights in Tamil Nadu, among others. The game show Home Sweet Home, shown on STAR Vijay in 2011, was a short appearance in his professional television hosting career. He has also acted as a brand ambassador for Pothys and Kumaran Jewelry Shop, among other organisations.
