Viral: Wedding Invite Of Varun Tej

The very beautiful Lavanya Tripathi is soon going to be the wife of Mega family hero Varun Tej. There were a few pre-wedding parties being hosted by Chiranjeevi and Allu Arjun already. The wedding is going to take place in a dreamy Italian ceremony on November 1st, and it will be just the family attending it.

Amid this, the wedding invite has come out on social media and is going viral now. The gorgeous wedding invite has the ‘VL’ emblem on the top of it. The invite has ‘Konidela’s Invitation’ followed by ‘With the blessings of Smt Anjana Devi & Late Sri Konidela Venkat Rao, Late Smt Satyavathi and Sri M Suryanarayana’.

The ‘Best Compliments from Smt & Sri Konidela Chiranjeevi, Smt & Sri Konidela Pawan Kalyan, Smt & Sri Konidela RamCharan’ part remains the highlight.

The actual invitation says, ‘Smt Padmaja & Sri Konidela Nagababu request the pleasure of your gracious presence on the occasion of the wedding reception of their beloved Son Varun Tej with Lavanya Tripathi (Daughter of Smt Kiran & Sri Deoraj Tripathi’.

The reception invite is designed in pink and it reads, ‘Reception – Sunday 05 November 2023’ and mentions the venue as N-Convention in Madhapur Hyderabad. The wedding reception will have the famous celebrities from film and politics as attendees.

The coctail party will be hosted by the couple on October 30th night in Tuscany, Italy. Nehendi and Haldi ceremony will take place on October 31st followed by the wedding ceremony on November 1st.
