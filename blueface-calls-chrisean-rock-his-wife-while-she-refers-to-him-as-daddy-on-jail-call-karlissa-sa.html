It has been about almost two months since Blueface's most recent arrest. Multiple reports said that it was a byproduct of Jaidyn Alexis and Chrisean Rock causing a stir. Them not being able to come forward about the things they did got their man put in jail. Those were the paraphrased words of the California rapper's mother, Karlissa Saffold. She has always had the staunch opinion that none of them deserve to be with her son. Her stance continues to grow stronger, after Blueface's recent jail call.

In the audio clips below, Blueface requests that a close friend of his to call Chrisean Rock. However, he does not refer to her as that. He asks to speak to his "wife." In the ensuing audio Rock continuously addresses him as "daddy" throughout Blueface's jail call and how much she was waiting for his phone call. This eventually got around quick enough for Karlissa to react with another "I told you so" tone.

Blueface's Jail Call Is Just "Jail Talk"

In the final clip, she calls out her son for calling her "wife" when he "won't even the let the baby call you daddy." In the end, she says that is just "another promotion for the circus" and that it is "a hot f*****g mess." Fans were in agreeance but still have trepidations about how many times she has been right.
