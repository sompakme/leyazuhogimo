Has First Dates Fred Sirieix had a hair transplant?

Spread the love             

Fred Sirieix, known for his warm welcome on Channel 4’s “First Dates,” is currently starring in the new season of “Gordon, Gino and Fred Go Greek!” Viewers tuning into the show’s latest season noticed a change in Fred’s appearance, particularly with his hair, sparking questions about whether he has had a hair transplant or made other changes to his hair. Let’s explore the details.

Who Is Fred Sirieix?

Fred Sirieix was born on January 27, 1972, making him currently 49 years old. Originally from France, Fred grew up in Limoges, where he received training to work in front of house positions at a Michelin-star restaurant. He first appeared on television screens in 2013 as the front of house at the Channel 4 First Dates restaurant. Fred has been a consistent presence on the show throughout all 16 seasons to date. He has also appeared on BBC’s “Two Million Pound Menu” and more recently on “Gordon, Gino and Fred’s Road Trip.”

Fred Sirieix’s Hair Transformation

Fans of Fred Sirieix were taken aback when they tuned in to the third series of “Gordon, Gino and Fred Go Greek!” and noticed a change in Fred’s appearance, particularly his hair. Known for his short, neat haircut, Fred was now sporting a darker and longer hairstyle.

While Fred has not publicly disclosed whether he underwent a hair transplant or made any other significant changes to his hair, it appears that he has simply chosen to adopt a new style and naturally grew out his hair. Some viewers only recently noticed this change, but Fred’s Instagram followers would know that he began sporting the longer hairstyle during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown.

One fan expressed their appreciation for the new look, saying, “I’m liking the longer hair Fred,” to which Fred responded that it’s a “lockdown aftereffect.”

What to Expect from “Gordon, Gino and Fred Go Greek!”

The third season of “Gordon, Gino and Fred Go Greek!” premiered with its first episode titled “Mamma Mia!” on September 27th. This season is a two-part road trip style program where the trio travels around the Greek Islands, including Mykonos, Santorini, Crete, and mainland Greece, visiting Athens, Meteora, and Attica.

The second episode, airing on October 4th on ITV at 9 PM, explores the wine regions of Greece while indulging in local delicacies.

If you’re a fan of the show or curious about Fred’s new look, be sure to catch the second episode of “Gordon, Gino and Fred Go Greek!” on October 4th.
