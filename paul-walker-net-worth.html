Paul Walker Net Worth

Paul Walker was an American actor born on September 12, 1973 in Glendale, CA. He was best known for his role as Brian O'Conner in The Fast and the Furious, and he reprised this role in five sequels. He also had prominent roles in Varsity Blues, Pleasantville, and The Lazarus Project. Unfortunately, he passed away in a car crash in November 2013 in Santa Clarita, California.
