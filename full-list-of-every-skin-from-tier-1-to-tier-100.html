Full list of every skin from Tier 1 to Tier 100

The much-awaited Fortnite Chapter 4 Season 1 Battle Pass is finally here. The new pass comes with an exciting mix of original skins and collaborations. Geralt of Rivia from The Witcher and Doom Slayer will be gracing the pass in the all-new season.

Besides popular collabs, Geno has finally been added to the Battle Royale game and will be available at Tier 93 of the Battle Pass. He comes with an amazing built-in emote that players can unlock after reaching Tier 100.

The Fortnite Chapter 4 Season 1 Battle Pass will also cost 950 V-Bucks, and players will end up getting 1,500 V-Bucks after completing the entire Pass.
