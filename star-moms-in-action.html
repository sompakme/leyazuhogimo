Star Moms in Action

Katie Holmes & daughter Suri

Mother and daughter sport some lucky green to get into the St. Patrick's Day spirit. It's a good thing Suri's being carried-her shoes seem to have been lost in the shuffle.

Angelina Jolie & daughter Zahara

Angelina and Zahara enjoy the Mardi Gras view from a balcony of their New Orleans home. The busy star is also mom to sons Maddox and Pax and younger daughter Shiloh.

Heidi Klum with daughter Leni & son Henry

The multilingual supermodel mom reads a picture book in German to two of her lovely little ones, Leni and Henry. Her third, Johan, joined the family last year.

Madonna with sons David and Rocco & daughter Lourdes

Madonna cradles her third child, David, as she makes her way through an airport shortly after his adoption. Her older kids-daughter Lourdes and son Rocco-complete this traveling band.

Gwen Stefani & son Kingston

Kingston-sporting a fancy mini mohawk-gets an early lesson in rock star style. And who better to learn from than the woman who defines it-his proud mama Gwen!

Liv Tyler & son Milo

The gorgeous pair are a perfect match in contrasting patterns as they make their way through Los Angeles International airport.

Reese Witherspoon & daughter Ava

Same hair, same stride, same snack craving! Though Reese's cone is chocolate and daughter Ava's is vanilla, there is no doubt that these two are family.

Gwyneth Paltrow & daughter Apple

Little Apple's wrapped snugly in a cheerful jacket as she keeps mom company on an errand run.

Kate Winslet & son Joe

Joe gets a little pick-me-up from mom as the two navigate New York City. Winslet's also a hands-on mom to daughter Mia.

Kate Hudson & son Ryder

Kate channels her inner child while enjoying a day at Sea World in Australia with Ryder.

Jennifer Garner & daughter Violet

The hands-on mom shares Violet's obvious excitement-and dimples-as they visit a park in Santa Monica, California.

Kelly Ripa with sons Joaquin & Michael

Ripa kicks back in New York's Central Park with sons (Michael, at right, and Joaquin in her lap). Perhaps she's waving to her daughter, Lola, who also joined the play date.

Sarah Jessica Parker & son James

The down-to-earth star carries groceries and her favorite little guy while out shopping in New York.

Kate Moss & daughter Lila

Out for a stroll in London, the fashionista matches her Hermes bag with her daughter's outfit. Perhaps Lila, who clearly knows how to pout for the camera, will follow in her mom's famous footsteps?

Courteney Cox & daughter Coco

Cox has her hands full with little Coco, out and about in Hollywood.
