Emily Ratajkowski Mastered the Mob Wife Aesthetic in Leather and Leopard

Carmela Soprano is suddenly everyone's new fashion muse. From Kendall Jenner's extravagant fluffy fur coat in Aspen to Rihanna and her head-to-toe leather look at Couture Week, celebrities are channeling the bold style of a mob wife with a new sense of sophistication. And Emily Ratajkowski is the latest A-lister to try out the trend. 

On Friday, the model took a walk on the wild side during one of her signature New York City strolls, wearing a striking combination of leather and leopard. For the daytime outing, Emily bundled up in a faded brown leather jacket that was purposefully oversized and matched her low-rise cheetah midi skirt below. She finished off the look with more leather in the form of black knee-high boots with a stiletto heel while accessorizing with a big black scarf and shield sunglasses. 

Rather than the over-the-top beauty mob wives are known for, EmRata took a more minimal approach, putting on little-to-no makeup and styling her dark brunette hair down and straight with a middle part. 

Just a few days earlier, Emily stepped out in leather again — but this time, her look was less crime boss and more model off-duty. Wearing a dark green leather bomber jacket over a turquoise hoodie, the street style star paired her cozy top layers with black yoga pants, white sneakers, and big sunglasses.
