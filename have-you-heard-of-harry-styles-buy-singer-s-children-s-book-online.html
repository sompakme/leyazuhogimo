'Have You Heard of Harry Styles?' Buy Singer's Children's Book Online

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

Harry Styles is truly a superstar for all ages. Case in point: this new Harry Styles children’s book, which introduces little ones to the singer, actor and style icon, though a fun and colorful format.

Officially titled Have You Heard of Harry Styles?, the book features interactive pages that kids can spin, flip and lift to reveal different looks and scenes from Styles’ life. The easy-to-read story, meantime, traces the artist’s career from singing in talent shows, to his time with One Direction, to traveling and touring all around the world.

Related Stories

Published by Silver Dolphin Books, the Harry Styles kids book is illustrated by Dublin-based artist Una Woods, whose work has appeared in multiple books and magazines over the years. Many of Styles’ most memorable looks are featured in the book, including his jumpsuit, his fuzzy pink coat from Coachella, and of course, his now-iconic feather boa. The colorful illustrations encourage readers to find their own eclectic tastes and personal style, just like Harry. As the publisher notes, “By celebrating Harry’s impact on popular culture, Have You Heard of Harry Styles is the perfect book to teach kids to make the world a better place by just being themselves.”

Though it’s designed for little ones 3-5 years of age (with thick and durable pages that hold up to repeated use), this is really a book for Harry Styles fans of all ages. Released February 6, the kids book is currently in the Top 10 of Amazon’s bestselling children’s books list (just behind a Taylor Swift book, natch). You can get Have You Heard of Harry Styles? on sale for just $10 right now on Amazon.com.

Have You Heard of Harry Styles? is the second book in this children’s series from Silver Dolphin, after Have You Heard of Dolly Parton? which was released last August.
