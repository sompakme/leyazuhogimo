Nigerian Man Builds Many Flats for Rent, Installs Borehole, Fences His Investment

  • A young Nigerian man got many applauding his investment move as he built a multi-flat house for rent
  • The young landlord thanked God for the blessing as people said that he must have spent millions of naira
  • A building engineer, Gbenga Muritala, who saw the house, said the owner must have spent heavily on it

A young Nigerian man has made a significant investment in his life by building a multi-tenement house.

He (@shawalin_) showed the moment the house was decked with iron rods and concerts. He supervised the project.

Flats for rents

The man painted the building to attract prospective tenants willing to rent flats. The interior had a POP design.

Nigerians celebrated his investment as some tried to guess how much he sunk into the big project.

"Though I can't say how much the person must have spent on the building project as I don't have the plan and access to materials used, it is clear that project must have gulped a lot of money. Making decking alone is no joke."
