Rihanna Does Business Casual in an Edgy Cutout Blazer &amp; Ball Cap

Leave it to Rihanna to remix business casual.

Yesterday, the pop star was photographed stepping out for a business meeting wearing a daring take on a typical workwear outfit. Sporting a pinstripe cutout blazer courtesy of Junya Watanabe, Rihanna also rocked a sultry satin lace tank top tucked into white shorts, paired with an edgy baseball cap by R13 and white pointed-toe mules by Luis De Javier. She further accessorized her look with layered gold necklaces and a strand of oversized pearls, small hoop earrings, and a logo-print bag by Maison Goyard.

BACKGRID

Rih's business outing comes after she wore a bright-pink vintage Dior slipdress while out with her boyfriend, rapper A$AP Rocky, last week. The pair have slowly gone more public with their relationship in recent weeks, being photographed heading to dinner and drink dates in and around New York City.

Rocky recently opened up to GQ about his relationship with Rihanna and how he considers her to be "the one."

"[It's] so much better," Rocky said of being coupled up and embracing monogamy with Rihanna. "So much better when you got the One. She amounts to probably, like, a million of the other ones. I think when you know, you know. She's the One."

The rapper also credited his girlfriend for keeping him creatively inspired, especially while filming and producing his upcoming documentary, Stockholm Syndrome.

"[It's] important having that support in real life, you know?" Rocky told Entertainment Tonight about RiRi's appearance in and her support for the new project. "It's amazing and I'm honestly truly blessed, for real."
