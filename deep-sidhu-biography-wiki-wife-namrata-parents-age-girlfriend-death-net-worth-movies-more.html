Deep Sidhu (born 2 April 1984 – Death 15 February 2022; Aged: 37 years old) was a well-known actor, advocate, social activist, politician, media face, internet influencer, and social media star from Sri Muktsar Sahib, Punjab, India. He was famously known in the country after his participation in the farmer’s protest in 2021. He was very courageous and supported farmers during the protest against the government for the cancellation of the three farms act.

Deep Sidhu Controversy and Arrest

As everyone knows, Deep Sidhu became the leader of the farmer protest because of his immense efforts. In February 2021, during the farmer protest, he along with many farmers went to Red Fort. As per the sources, he hoisted the religious flag at Red Fort on the day of Republic Day (26 January 2021).

On 9 February 2021, Deep was arrested by the police for fueling violent activities at the Red Fort. Later, he got bailed after the court approved his plea. Later, Deep was again arrested by the Archaeological Survey of India for damaging monuments and vandalism by the rioters. On 16 April 2021, he was released from jail on bail.
