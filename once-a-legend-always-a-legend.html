Once a Legend, Always a Legend

Brahmanandam is undoubtedly one of the best actors in Indian cinema. Though he is widely known for comic roles, there is a hidden serious and dramatic actor inside him. After a long time, director Krishna Vamshi unleashed that side of Brahmanandam in Ranga Marthanda.

Starring Prakash Raj and Ramya Krishna in the lead roles, Ranga Marthanda was released the other day. Both Prakash Raj and Brahmanandam played the roles of theatre artists in the film. Brahmanandam played an intense and serious role in the movie.

He unleashed his incredible acting skills in one of the emotional scenes of the film. People would be mesmerized and surprised to see Brahmanandam in such an intense role. 

Earlier, Brahmanandam played these kinds of roles in films like Babai Hotel and Amma. But he didn’t play such roles in the last three decades. So, the present generation audiences were shocked to see and know that he can pull off serious roles with a lot of intensity. 

Meanwhile, Ranga Marthanda is having a poor run at the box office due to the lack of a big star cast and weak promotions. We have to see if the situation betters in the coming days due to the positive word of mouth.
