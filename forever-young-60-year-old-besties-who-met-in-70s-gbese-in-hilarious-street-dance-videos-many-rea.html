Two lovely grannies have been blessings netizens with cute dance videos as they prove that there is no age too old not to enjoy yourself.

DaBabyBommers, as they are known on social media platforms, are a group of two 60-year-old besties who have been friends since 1978.

As their name suggests, the two are huge fans of US rapper DaBaby and become famous after a TikTok video of them dancing to his song Rockstar went viral on social media.
