Alona Tal Bio, Age, Husband, Height, Net Worth, Family

Alona Tal is an Israeli-born actress, singer and singer. She is best known to viewers for her recurring roles on hit TV series like Veronica Mars and Supernatural. The multi-talented star also has multiple film credits to her name across various genres. Beyond her thriving acting career, Tal is also known to be a talented singer and she recorded many of her original songs before choosing to focus on acting.

Alona Tal, Bio, Age

Alona Tal was born on October 20, 1983 in Herzliya, a city on the central coast of Israel. Her mother Ayala (née Sabat) is a lawyer while her father Ami Tal is an IT professional. She has Turkish Jewish ancestry on her father’s side. Information regarding her early years of education is not available, but we do know that she became interested in acting at an early age. However, she only started her acting career after two years of compulsory military service, according to the Israel Defense Service Law.

Career

In 2002, Alona Tal got her first role in the movie Isreali In the Magic Castle (Original title: Pim Pam Po BeArmon HaKsamim ) and the following year she appeared in another movie To Be a Star (Original title: Lihiyot Kochav ). She finally got her breakout role on the television sitcom HaPijamot (2003-2004, 2006). The sitcom was based on a music group that was struggling to advance in the music industry and thus allowed Alona Tal to show her musical side.

Following her success on the sitcom HaPijamot, Tal began working as a recording artist even as she continued her budding acting career. She recorded a song with rapper Isreali Subliminal before eventually moving to the United States. She continued her musical career in the United States working with international music star Wyclef Jean.

Alona Tal’s first acting role in the United States was in the drama series Veronica Mars (2004-05) featuring a recurring character, Meg Manning. She also had the chance to star in several television series, including CSI: Crime Scene Investigation (2005), Case Closed (2006) and 7th Heaven (2006). She also portrayed recurring character Jo Harvelle in the fantasy series Supernatural (2006-11). The talented actress was also part of the main cast of the short-lived drama series Cane (2007).

After numerous guest roles, Tal landed another starring role in the drama series Cult (2013) which only lasted one season. Also in 2013, she appeared in the final season of the series Burn Notice, starring as a recurring character Sonya. The following year, she landed a spot in the main cast of the web series. Hand of God (2014-17) with Jocelyn Harris and she also starred in the drama series Isreali Hostages (2016). In 2017, the talented actress was chosen to play Stella in the first season of the military series. SEAL Team. After a successful first season, SEAL Teamwas renewed for a second season which debuted in late 2018.

Beyond her successes on the small screen, Alona Tal has also scored several big screen credits since her transition to Hollywood. She had roles in movies like Taking 5 (2007), Half Past Dead 2 (2007), University (2008), Undocumented (2010), Kalamity (2010), City Destroyed (2013) and Saving Madison (2014) . The talented actress also lent her voice to a role in the animated horror film Night of the Living Dead: Darkest Dawn (2015) and she also appeared in the musical comedy film Opening Night ( 2016).

family, husband

Alona Tal is also enjoying a happy family life besides her thriving Hollywood career. The Isreali-born actress has been married to American actor Marcos Ferraez for more than ten years. The duo tied the knot on March 3, 2005 and have one child together. They welcomed their first child on March 7, 2017, a girl named Charlie Ferraez.
