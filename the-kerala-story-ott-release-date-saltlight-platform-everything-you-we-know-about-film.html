The Kerala Story OTT Release Date, Saltlight Platform: Everything You We Know About Film

The Kerala Story” has created quite a buzz, becoming a blockbuster at the box office. Now, fans are eager to know when this exciting film will hit the OTT platform for them to enjoy. In this article, we have all the essential details you need to know about the movie’s release.

The Blockbuster Debut

Box Office Sensation On May 5, 2023, “The Kerala Story” made its grand entrance into theaters, surprising everyone by outperforming James Gunn’s “Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3” on its very first day. Despite mixed reviews due to its unique storyline, this movie managed to rake in a staggering Rs 35 crore in just three days. The film’s enthusiastic reception hints at a promising box office journey and a notable theatrical run. Now, fans eagerly await its release on OTT platforms.

A Glimpse at the Movie

Plot and Cast Details Directed by Sudipto Sen, “The Kerala Story” boasts a talented ensemble cast, including Adah Sharma, Yogita Bihani, Sonia Balani, Siddhi Idnani, Vijay Krishna, Pranay Pachauri, and Pranav Mishra. This social-drama unfolds in Hindi and is set to release in Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, and Telugu languages. With an intriguing plot based on a true incident, the film delves into the lives of 32 young women who mysteriously disappear in Kerala.

Platform and Release Date

OTT Release Anticipation Fans have been eagerly awaiting the OTT release of “The Kerala Story.” According to sources, the OTT rights for this movie have been acquired by ZeeWhile no official confirmation exists, it is rumored that the film may debut on the OTT platform approximately 4-6 weeks after its theatrical release, expected in the final week of June. However, with the movie’s current success in theaters, an early OTT release seems unlikely.

The Controversial Narrative

Addressing Criticisms “The Kerala Story” is a 2023 drama film directed by Vipul Amrutlal Shah. It features Adah Sharma, Yogita Bihani, Sonia Balani, and Siddhi Idnani in pivotal roles. The film revolves around a group of Keralan women who are allegedly forced to join the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS) and convert to Islam. The movie has stirred controversy by making claims of hundreds of Hindu women from Kerala being converted to Islam and recruited by ISIS, based on the contentious theory of “love jihad.”

Mixed Reviews and Public Reaction

Controversy and Criticism Despite its box office success, “The Kerala Story” has faced criticism from various quarters. It became the third-highest-grossing Hindi movie of 2023 with a global gross of 303.68 crore (US$38 million). However, critics have accused the film of promoting Islamophobia and referred to it as propaganda. Legal battles and protests have followed its release, especially in Kerala, West Bengal, and Tamil Nadu.

A Thought-Provoking Drama

Impact on Audiences “The Kerala Story” has sparked intense discussions and varying opinions online. While some viewers consider it a factual narrative, others view it as propaganda. Regardless, the film’s thought-provoking and emotionally charged storyline continues to captivate audiences. Director Sudipto Sen, along with the exceptional cast, has created a film that promises to leave a lasting impact.

Conclusion:

As we eagerly await “The Kerala Story’s” OTT release, it’s clear that this movie has left an indelible mark on the audience. Its intriguing plot, stellar cast, and controversial themes have made it one of the most talked-about films of the year. Stay tuned for more updates on this much-anticipated release.
