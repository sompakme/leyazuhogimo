The exact products Natalie Portman is wearing on our September 2019 issue

Natalie Portman discusses the power of women in the September issue of Harper's Bazaar, on newsstands from 2 August, wearing the most iconic shade of lipstick: a striking bright red.

Dressed head-to-toe in Dior, the actress' mouth models the new Rouge Dior Ultra Care lipstick, in the brand's unashamedly bold signature shade '999 Bloom'.

On sale now, this innovative formula offers intense pigment pay-off and long-wear, with a light texture that's easy to apply.

Balancing out the power lip, Portman – a long-serving face of Dior beauty – wears a soft smoky brown eye, courtesy of the the Dior Backstage Eye Palette in '003 Amber Neutrals', with Diorshow Iconic Overcurl mascara in '090 Over Noir' on her lashes. In the waterlines, Diorshow Khôl in '529 Beige Khôl' – a flesh tone to neutralise redness – gently opens up her eyes. The Diorshow Brow Styler in '001 Universal Brown' further densified her defined brows.
